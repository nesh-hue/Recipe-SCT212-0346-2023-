WHOLE WHEAT PANCAKES

INGRIDIENTS

  • 3/4 cup of wheat flour

  • 3/4 cup whole wheat flour

  • 1 cup water

  • 3/4 tsp salt

  • 1/2 sachet saf-instant yeast 11g

  • 1 tsp sugar

  • 2 tps vegetable oil

  • Magarine or cocnut oil or milk

  • honey

  • Baking powder

STEPS TO FOLLOW

  1. Pour the lukewarm water into a large mixing bowl and mix the yeast

  2. Add the wheat flour,whole flour,salt,sugar,softened butter or magarine and baking powder.Mix until the dough has an even concictency.

  3. Knead for 5 minutes

  4. Shape the dough into a ball.Cover and leave to rest for 10 minutes.

  5. Once the dough has risen,cut it into 8 equal pieces.

  6. shape them into balls.Leave to rest for 5 minutes.

  7. Flatten the pieces until they are around 12cm in diameter,then put then on a tea towel.

  8. Cover and leave to rise for 10 minute.

  9. Put a little oil in the pan.Cook over a medium heat for 4 minutes,turning every minute.

  10. Make sure all pancakes don't overcook

  11. Pour out honey sparringly to each pancake

tips

  • Use fresh milk for better results.
  • Watch out while rolling to allow circular dough.

    • Enjoy your pancakes!
